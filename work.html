If you receive a message to access your Fall courses, follow the link to the new MAX platform. You can also access that platform directly at https://itumax.instructure.com. When logging in to MAX for the first time, your username will be your complete ITU email address, and your default password will be your complete ITU email address with your year of birth appended to the end. For example:

Username: doejane1234@students.itu.edu

Password: doejane1234@students.itu.edu1994

 

Please make sure to update your password using Reset Password as soon as you have logged in for the first time.

Also, MAX is available on your phone! Search your App store for Canvas Student, download the app and enter ITUMAX to download the ITU student app: Here is a complete link to downloading the phone app: https://community.canvaslms.com/docs/DOC-9831-18561185379.
